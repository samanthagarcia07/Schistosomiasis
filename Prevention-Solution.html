Prevention/Solution




Individual Behavioral Prevention

Since Schistosomiasis is a water-borne disease the best ways to prevent infection are:

  • Avoid swimming/drinking out of contaminated of bodies of freshwater.
  • Bring contaminated freshwater to a boil of 145 degrees for 15-20 seconds.
  • Vigorious towel drying ay help after brief skin exposure of contaminated water.


    Environmental Prevention

    Schistosomiasis is spread through water containing schistomes from feces. We can prevent this by implementing a plumbing systems where Schistosomiasis continuously happens. With the plumbing systems people in underdeveloped countries won't throw their waste into rivers and lakes and hinder the spread of Schistosomiasis.
